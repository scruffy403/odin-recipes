Cake Recipe

Description

This is a basic chocolate cake recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup canola oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Mix until smooth. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
  3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool completely.
